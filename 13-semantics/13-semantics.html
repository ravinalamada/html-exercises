A definition

markup

/mɑ:kʌp/
noun

noun:markup; plural noun: markups; noun: mark-up; plural noun:mark-ups

  1. the amount added to the cost price of goods to cover overheads and price.

    2. a markup of 50 per cent

  2. the process or results of correcting text in preparation for printing.

  3. COMPUTING
    a set of tags assigned to elements of a text to indicate their relation to rest of the text or dictate how they should be displayed.

    4. markup languages
